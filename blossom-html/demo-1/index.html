Who Are We

My Pulse Fund is a dynamic venture studio dedicated to transforming early-stage brilliance into enduring success. We do more than just provide capital, we act as a partner, mentor, and ally for founders to grow.

Its foundation initiative, Pulse Fund I, is the first fund under My Pulse Fund— a SEBI-registered Category II Alternative Investment Fund (AIF) focused on strategic investments across real estate, startups, and SMEs, with a special emphasis on Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities driving India’s next wave of growth.

Designed with a balanced investment strategy, Pulse Fund I not only fuels high-potential ventures but also ensures capital preservation and steady growth for investors through diversified asset allocation. The fund aims to create a win-win ecosystem, empowering founders with growth capital and strategic guidance, while offering investors risk-mitigated, long-term value creation opportunities across emerging sectors

Our Unique Value Proposition

At Pulse Fund I, our approach goes beyond capital. We combine structured investment discipline with hands-on support to create lasting value for both founders and investors.

Purpose-Driven Capital: Flexible, founder-aligned structures with a long-term commitment that extends beyond funding.

Data-Driven Execution: Rigorous due diligence and analytics-led decision-making, with performance tracked across the investment lifecycle.

Unmatched Ecosystem Access : Integrated legal, financial, branding, and talent support, backed by partnerships with accelerators, VCs, and institutional players.

Expert Mentorship: Guidance from seasoned entrepreneurs, ex-bankers, and technologists, providing strategic insights, scaling roadmaps, and network access.
